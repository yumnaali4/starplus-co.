Quality Managment

Star plus Pharma recognizes its responsibility owards meeting customer requirements and satisfaction by applying quality criteria in all business aspects within the organization.
Star plus Pharma utilizes state-of-the-art technologies in order to manufacture safe and effective high-quality medicinal products. heavy investment in advanced machinery/technology has served in efficiency in product life cycle and supply-chain managment.
Star plus Pharma is committed to protect its employees as well as the enviroment .The company relies on the participation of all employees on the continuous improvement of the Quality,Health,Safety and Enviromental Managment System (QHSE0).





Star plus Pharma Quality Managment System (QMS) is focused on achieving quality policy and quality objectives to meet the regulatory and customer requirenents.
The QMS is being maintained and monitored using electronic record that improves the workflow within the quality system.
Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) -SAP, streamline business application and provide a high degree of confidence on managing critical quality /GMP processes.
Manufacturing Execution System (MES) it covers all areas related to specification,manufacturing,quality assurance,and process optimization .The system is benefiting in accelerating the time of batch release, improved process quality just in time and maintining the master batch record.
